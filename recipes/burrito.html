Burrito

Description

A breakfast burrito bursting with flavor, this hearty dish wraps up scrambled eggs, savory ground beef, and melted cheese in a warm flour tortilla. The combination of rich, creamy eggs and seasoned beef creates a perfect balance, while the cheese adds a gooey, comforting texture. Vibrant bell peppers bring a slight crunch and a touch of sweetness, while golden, crispy potatoes give the burrito a satisfying bite, adding both flavor and heft.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tortilla
  • Red peppers
  • Cheese
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Heat up ground beef
  2. Heat up eggs
  3. Heat up potatos
  4. Mix ingredients and roll
