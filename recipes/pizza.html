Pizza

Description

Cheese pizza is a classic favorite enjoyed by people of all ages around the world. With its simple yet satisfying combination of a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and a generous layer of melted cheese—typically mozzarella—it offers a comforting and familiar flavor. The balance between the tangy sauce and the creamy, gooey cheese creates a delicious harmony that makes cheese pizza a go-to option for any occasion. Whether served fresh out of the oven or enjoyed as leftovers, it remains a timeless staple in the world of comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Pizza sauce

Steps

  1. Make dough
  2. Put pizza sauce on dough
  3. Put cheese on dough
  4. Bake
