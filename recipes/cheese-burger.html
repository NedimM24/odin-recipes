Cheese Burger

Descriprion

A cheeseburger is the ultimate comfort food, combining a juicy, tender beef patty with the irresistible melt of cheese. The savory richness of the patty pairs perfectly with a slice of gooey cheese, usually cheddar, American, or Swiss, adding a creamy, tangy layer to every bite. Sandwiched between a soft, toasted bun, it's often topped with classic condiments like ketchup, mustard, and pickles, along with fresh lettuce, tomato, and onions for a burst of crunch and freshness. The combination of textures—from the soft bun to the charred patty and melted cheese—creates a satisfying and flavorful experience that never goes out of style

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beef patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Grill patty
  2. Place patty on bun
  3. Place the rest of the ingredinets on the patty
